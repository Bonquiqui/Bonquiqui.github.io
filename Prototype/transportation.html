Transportation Around Taniti

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay once per week. Taniti is served by a small airport accommodating jets and propeller planes, with expansion underway to welcome larger aircraft in the coming years. Once on the island, getting around is easy — public buses, taxis, rental cars, and bikes are all available.

Public Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. daily. Private buses serve the rest of the island.

Bike Rentals
