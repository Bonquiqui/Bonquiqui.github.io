Entertainment in Taniti

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and visit the volcano. However, there is plenty more to discover from chartered fishing tours and snorkeling to helicopter rides, art galleries, live music, and a new dance club. Many activities are centered in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.
